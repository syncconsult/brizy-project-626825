Questions?

Good news. We've got answers. Here are some of the common questions.

What is CryptoPros.biz?


CryptoPros.biz is an advertising and marketing tools platform that provides participants with the ability to earn commissions in the system by referring others to CryptoPros.biz

What do I get by purchasing at CryptoPros.biz?


Each Bronze purchase provides the member with a 14-day banner and text ad. Our Platinum Subscription provides unlimited view Text and Banner ads that never expire if the subscription remains active. The member receives a new banner and text ad with each subscription renewal.


Are there benefits to making referrals?


Yes, your income will grow faster and you will also earn a 100% direct referral matrix matching bonus in the Bronze Matrix and one time $25 Sponsor Bonuses in the Platinum Matrix.


What is the Bronze direct referral matching bonus and Platinum sponsor bonus?


You will earn a 100% match of the matrix income generated by each of your direct referrals. If your referral earns $100 in matrix income you also earn $100. With the Platinum Subscription, we pay a one-time sponsor bonus if the direct sponsor also has a position in the Platinum Matrix.


What payment methods do you accept?


We accept Bitcoin, Ether, and Litecoin.


Why don’t you accept credit cards or Paypal?


Both credit cards and Paypal are expensive to accept and have the risk of chargeback which puts the company at risk. Cryptocurrencies are “Non-recourse” meaning there is no risk of chargeback and loss to the company.


How and how often do you pay commissions?


Commissions are paid daily (M-F) within 24-48 hours of request via Bitcoin, Ether or Litecoin. If the company has a shortage of one coin and a surplus of a particular coin we may request you accept the commission payment in a different coin; such as Bitcoin instead of Litecoin, to avoid high exchange fees.


What is the minimum withdrawal?


$25.00


Is there a withdrawal fee?


Yes, the withdrawal fee is $3.00 to cover the fees that are charged to us by our payment processor, coinpayments.net. When Coinpayments fees rise above $3.00 we charge the actual fee that Coinpayments charges Crypto Pros to process a payment.


Please explain the wallet system in Crypto Pros?


The member will see four wallets in their back office. The Purchase Wallet, Cash Wallet, Repurchase Wallet, and Subscription Wallet.


Purchase Wallet: All deposited funds will deposit into the Purchase Wallet. Purchase Wallet funds can only be used to purchase Bronze ad packs. Platinum Subscriptions and to PIF other members.


Cash Wallet: 75% of all earned Bronze Matrix and the Bronze Matching Bonus funds will deposit into the members Cash Wallet. If the Platinum Subscription Wallet has a balance of $25 or more 100% of all Platinum Matrix and Bonus funds will be deposited into the Cash Wallet and can be withdrawn or used to PIF other members.


Repurchase Wallet: 25% of all matrix and matching bonus will deposit into the Repurchase Wallet. The Repurchase Wallet will be automated and set to purchase additional Bronze ad packs for the member once the balance of the Repurchase Wallet exceeds $2.


Subscription Wallet: the first $25 of funds from the Platinum Matrix and Platinum One-time Bonus is held in the Subscription to allow for automated renewal of the Platinum Subscription.
